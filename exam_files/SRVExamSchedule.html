Exam Schedule

If you have a daytime exam clash, you should immediately contact your professors to determine if an alternate writing time is available. If this cannot be established, you must complete the appropriate form:

  • UBC Vancouver students should complete the Daytime Exam Clash form available at Student Information Services in Brock Hall.

  • UBC Okanagan students should complete the Request for Out-of-Time form available from the appropriate Faculty Office.


    • Forms will be accepted up to one month prior to the exam date. Clashing exams will be scheduled back to back on the original exam date.
    Note: This schedule is subject to change. Changes to the schedule will usually not occur after April 12, 2024.

    Classroom details for December exams will be added in mid-November. Classroom details for April exams will be added in mid-March.
